Account Management

Our account management department maintain the links between our clients and the entire agency, some of their roles include:

• Ensuring departments are on track according to the client's needs.

• Leading agency presentations and ensuring that the ideas presented remain faithful to the brief, timeframe and budget.

• Managing, monitoring, reviewing and delivering advertisement projects Weekly reporting of Progress/Status Report.

• Sharing daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly & yearly media reports (If contracted).

• Liaising with the production team on client production requirements, including but not limited to TVC and radio production, showroom and exhibition. branding, shop signs, events management, Outdoor printing, promotional materials, merchandising.

Production

The productions department handles all of our operational executions and are responsible for:

• Directing all tasks and staff involved in producing print & audiovisual campaigns.

• Overseeing print production aspects of various media, including digital advertising, promotions, displays, exhibits, TV & radio commercials.

• Commissioning competitive pricings from third party suppliers directly to the client.

• Sourcing various external vendors and suppliers such for printing, display production, photography, etc.

• Supervising and attending events, trade shows and exhibitions to ensure all client needs are met.

• Highlighting all production obstacles/complications to the project art director and providing operational solutions and workarounds.

• Exploration of new production/ execution techniques.

Creative & Art

Once all the necessary client data is collated by our strategic planners - with the support of the accounts team our dedicated team of creatives which include; designers, copywriters, art and photography directors start to formulate their creative concept. The creative teams roles include:

• Generating creative ideas and concepts to fulfill the clients brief.

• Producing TV storyboards, print media roughs/scamps when communicating ideas to the client.

• Commissioning the necessary photographers, artists and filmmakers for each projects.

• Visiting and assessing locations for potential shoots.

• Working in editing suites when overseeing the final product.

Digital Production

We are define as the process by which creative ideas and assets (images, text and interactive apps) are translated into an array of digital media - websites, banner ads, rich media applications, HTML emails, mobile and social media applications so that the right messages are delivered via the right platform. The digital revolution has moved marketers closer than ever to realizing their ultimate fantasy - the ability to target specific messages to specific people at specific moments and places. And with tens of millions, even hundreds of millions, of global consumers reachable of whom we are ready to engage through a wide range of platforms and devices, the power of effective digital marketing seems limitless.
